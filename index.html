INDUSTRY FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Expert technical answers regarding adhesive systems, bulk purchase setups, and custom chemistry formulations.

What is the shelf life stability of REAL BOND® Industrial PVA Adhesives?

Typically, our standard water-based emulsions and synthesized copolymers remain highly stable for a period of 6 to 12 months when stored inside cool, dry rooms in original hermetic HDPE drums. Always seal covers tightly when not actively dispensing to avoid superficial drying patterns.

Can you tailor the formulation viscosity parameters for automatic labeling lines?

Absolutely. Our engineering staff regularly fine-tunes formulation viscosity levels (ranging anywhere from 500 cPs up to 14,000 cPs) and active drying parameters to match individual production velocities and roller mechanism dynamics.

What is the standard MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) for direct factory shipping?

For pilot testing and line integration assessment, we provide trial sizes starting from single 50 Kg plastic drums. Standard commercial production freight shipping starts from 500 Kg to multi-ton bulk container arrays.
